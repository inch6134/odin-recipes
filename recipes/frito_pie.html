Frito Pie

Description

"Living in the south you find these at football games, Sonic Drive Inn, and just about any fair that you attend. Very tasty and even better with a squirt of mustard."

Ingredients

  • 1 cup corn chips
  • 1 cup prepared chili
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tsp chopped onions (optional)
  • mustard (optional)

Steps

  1. Assemble by heating chili until warm either in a pot on the stove or in a microwave.
  2. Place corn chips in a bowl, pour over chili then top with the cheese, and onions if desired.
  3. Finish with a squirt of mustard.
